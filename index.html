BLOG SECTION

Demon Slayer (Kimetsu no Yaiba) is a manga by Koyoharu Gotouge. It follows Tanjiro Kamado, who becomes a demon slayer to avenge his family and cure his sister Nezuko, turned into a demon. Set in the Taisho era, it's famed for its compelling characters, artwork, and action, gaining worldwide popularity
Solo Leveling is a South Korean web novel by Chugong. It follows Sung Jin-Woo, a low-ranked hunter who gains the ability to level up and grow stronger after a near-death experience. His mundane life turns into an epic adventure filled with danger, mystery, and power as he hunts monsters solo.
Naruto is a Japanese manga series by Masashi Kishimoto. It follows Naruto Uzumaki, a young ninja with dreams of becoming Hokage, the leader of his village. Despite being shunned for harboring the Nine-Tails, a powerful demon, Naruto's determination and strength grow as he faces various challenges. His journey is marked by epic battles, deep friendships, and the quest for recognition and peace.
One Piece is a Japanese manga series by Eiichiro Oda. It follows Monkey D. Luffy, a young pirate with the ability to stretch his body like rubber after eating a Devil Fruit. His dream of finding the ultimate treasure, One Piece, leads him on an epic adventure filled with danger, mystery, and powerful enemies as he assembles a diverse and loyal crew.
